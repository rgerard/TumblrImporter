Migrate

To move your blog from Weebly to Tumblr, you will have to do the following three actions:

  1. Open a new blog with Tumblr.
  2. Find and record the link to the RSS feed of your Weebly blog.
  3. Record the domain of your new Tumblr blog.

Need help finding the link to your Weebly RSS feed?

To find the link to the RSS feed of your Weebly blog, go to your blog and search for the word "RSS". Typically there is an icon or link with this text. If you click on the RSS link, you should be taken to the url of your blog's RSS feed.

If you can't find the link on your blog, try typing in this link: http://www.yourblog.com/1/feed, but please replace "www.yourblog.com" with the real domain of your blog. This is a typical RSS feed link for a Weebly blog.

Once you have the required information, you're ready to begin!
